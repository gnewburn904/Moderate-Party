Our Platform

We focus on five core issues that Americans care about the most, and offer commonsense solutions to difficult problems.

The Economy

Free markets work best when they're truly competitive. We support free trade that benefits workers and consumers, smart deregulation that removes barriers to entrepreneurship, and policies that "unrig" the economy by breaking up monopolies and reducing cronyism. A dynamic, competitive economy creates opportunities for everyone on Main Street.

üè•

Health Care

Quality healthcare starts with more healthcare workers. We'll invest in training programs to dramatically increase the number of doctors and nurses nationwide, reducing wait times and improving care. We'll also learn from recent challenges to build robust pandemic preparedness systems, ensuring we're never caught unprepared by the next public health crisis.

üí∞

Cost of Living

Americans are squeezed by high costs for housing, healthcare, childcare, and education. We embrace an abundance agenda: build more housing where people want to live, reduce occupational licensing barriers that limit competition, reform zoning laws that restrict supply, and eliminate regulations that drive up costs without improving safety. More supply means lower prices and better opportunities.

üëÆ

Public Safety

Every community deserves to feel safe. We'll hire 100,000 additional police officers nationwide to strengthen community policing, improve response times, and build trust between law enforcement and the neighborhoods they serve. Effective policing combined with smart criminal justice reform creates safer streets while respecting civil liberties.

üóΩ

Immigration

America has always been a nation of immigrants, and we remain one. We welcome everyone who comes peacefully and wants to contribute to our society. A fair, efficient immigration system that processes people quickly, welcomes peaceful contributors, and enforces the law against criminals serves everyone's interests and honors our heritage as a land of opportunity.
